Facts about Banner House

The Banner House is located at 11 Stanley Close, Greenhithe, Kent DA9 9BG in a newly refurbished, spacious, and comfortable 2-bedroom apartment in Dartford, Kent. The apartment house has two bedrooms, one double, one single, and one full bathroom. The house has a restriction free onsite parking space. It is located at a walking distance to local shops, restaurants, gyms, and parks. It is an ideal location suited to professionals, friends, and families.

Local Amenities:

The House has free WiFi and is located close to Midnight Pharmacy (0.7miles) and Stone Pharmacy (0.3miles). There are local taxi services nearby to town and to the Airport.

Popular landmarks nearby include, but not limited to sites such as Darent Valley Hospital, Bluewater Shopping Centre, Stone Crossing, Ebbsfleet International and Dartford Stations. .
