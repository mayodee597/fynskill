An Overview of House Rules.

Guests are encouraged to park their vehicles in the back of the building or in the free street parking area on Stanley Close. No loud music is permitted during stay between 10pm and 8am. You are encouraged to remove your shoes inside the property so as not to ruin the wood flooring or carpets.
Guests are also encouraged to eat in the designated dining areas so as to avoid accidental spillages. No pets are allowed within this property neither overnight visitors without prior approval by your hosts.
There is a small charge of 25 pounds per person per night for additional guests over and above the allowed 3 guests. The hosts will provide Airbeds and sleeping mattresses if needed. No smoking allowed within the premises under any condition.
The property is also a drug-free zone and the internet is monitored 24/7. Inform the hosts of any property damage and they will be promptly replaced. Airbnb can claim up to 1000 pounds security deposit to offset major damages or breakages.
Please return all keys on checking out. There is a base charge of 250 pounds to change key locks and security keys and a charge of 15 pounds for damages to individual keys. On checkout, please strip all beddings, towels, and blankets and put into laundry baskets.
Close all windows and check for any forgotten belongings. Put house keys in the lockbox and exit the property on or before 10am checkout time. Please send message that the property has been vacated. Note that the bin emptying day is every Wednesday and bins must be put out between 4pm on Tuesday and 7am on Wednesday morning. Please note that the refuse bins are collected weekly while the recycle bins bi-weekly. Enjoy!
